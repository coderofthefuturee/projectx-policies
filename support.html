How do I create an account?

Download the Project X app from the App Store and follow the signup process. You'll need to provide basic information including your email, username, and agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

How do I report inappropriate content or users?

Use the report button (flag icon) on any post, comment, event, or user profile. Select a reason for reporting and provide additional details if needed. Our moderation team reviews all reports.

How do I purchase event tickets?

Browse events in the app, select an event you're interested in, and follow the purchase flow. Payments are processed securely through Stripe. You'll receive a confirmation email and ticket QR code.

How do I request music at an event?

While at an event, use the music request feature to suggest songs. Event organizers can view and play requested songs.

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password.

Yes. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your full payment card information on our servers.

How do I update the app?

Go to the App Store on your device, search for "Project X," and tap "Update" if an update is available. You can also enable automatic updates in your device settings.
